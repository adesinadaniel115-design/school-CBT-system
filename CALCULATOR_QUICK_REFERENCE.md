## Tips & Tricks

1. **Order of Operations**: Expressions are calculated using standard mathematical order (PEMDAS)
    - Parentheses first
    - Exponents/Powers
    - Multiplication & Division (left to right)
    - Addition & Subtraction (left to right)

2. **Decimal Precision**: Results are rounded to 10 decimal places to avoid floating-point errors

3. **Trig Functions**: All trigonometric functions (sin, cos, tan) use DEGREES, not radians
    - sin(90) = 1 (90 degrees)
    - Not sin(1.57) which would use radians

4. **Error Handling**:
    - Invalid expressions display "Error"
    - Division by zero shows "Error"
    - Keep calculations reasonable to avoid "Infinity"

5. **Factorial**: Only works with non-negative integers
    - 5! = 120
    - -5! = Not allowed

6. **Parentheses**: Required for grouping, especially with operations
    - (5 + 3) Ã— 2 = 16
    - 5 + 3 Ã— 2 = 11 (follows order of operations)
