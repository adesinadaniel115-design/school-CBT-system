## üÜò Troubleshooting

### "Calculator button is not showing"

- Scroll down in the sidebar
- The button is between Questions and Legend
- Refresh your page if it still doesn't appear

### "Calculator won't open"

- Check your browser console for errors
- Try a different web browser
- Ask your exam proctor for assistance

### "Calculations are showing wrong results"

- Check order of operations (PEMDAS)
- Use parentheses to be specific
- Example: 5 + 3 √ó 2 = 11 (multiply first)
- With parentheses: (5 + 3) √ó 2 = 16

### "Keyboard shortcuts don't work"

- Make sure the calculator is open (visible on screen)
- Try using the mouse buttons instead
- Some keyboard shortcuts might conflict with exam shortcuts

### "The display is too small"

- Zoom in on your browser (Ctrl + + on Windows)
- Make calculator window larger
- Try full-screen mode

## üí° Tips & Tricks

1. **Fast Calculation**: For repeated calculations, clear with C and start fresh
2. **Complex Expressions**: Use parentheses liberally to ensure correct order
3. **Check Your Work**: Calculate twice to verify important results
4. **Decimal Places**: The calculator shows up to 10 decimal places
5. **Negative Numbers**: Subtraction (‚àí) creates negatives in expressions
6. **Degrees vs Radians**: Trig functions use degrees (90¬∞ = 1), not radians
